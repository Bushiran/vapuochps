COMMUNITY HEALTH MANAGEMENT COMMITTEE(CHMC)

The Comunity Health Management Committee (CHMC) is a group of individuals who come together to oversee and guide the Comunity health initiatives. The Commitee's primary goal is to ensure that healthcare services are accessible, equitable, and meet the needs of the community.

The CHMC typically comprises:

  1. Community leaders
  2. Healthcare Professionals
  3. Public health experts
  4. Local government representatives
  5. Community members

The CHMC responsibilities may include:

  1. Assessing community health needs
  2. Developing health programs and services
  3. Coordinating healthcare services
  4. Monitoring program effectiveness
  5. Advocating for healthcare policy changes
  6. Fundraising and resource mobilization
  7. Community engagement and education

The CHMC plays a vital role in ensuring that community health needs are addressed, and that healthcare services are tailored to meet the unique needs of the community.
